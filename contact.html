Want To Get in Touch?

READERS, FELLOW TRAVELLERS AND BLOGGERS

I'm always thrilled to connect with my readers! Whether my narratives spark inspiration, you have ideas for topics I haven't explored, you wish to share a travel suggestion, or you simply want to say hi, feel free to reach out. My inbox is constantly growing, and though I may not always manage to respond promptly, please be assured that I carefully read each email from my readers. Your love and support mean the world to me!


CAMPAIGNS AND COLLABORATIONS

I welcome discussions about engaging collaboration possibilities with tourism boards, travel firms, and distinctive accommodations, as well as opportunities for speaking engagements and consulting. I have a preference for solo travel, where I meticulously plan my own itinerary, rely on public transport, and immerse myself in authentic local experiences to truly understand the essence of a destination. It's important to note that I don't undertake work, consultations, or writing assignments without compensation. Feel free to explore my Travel Writing and Blogging or Speaking page for additional information, and you can reach out via email to receive my media kit.
