But Why?

I firmly believe in the power of tourism to drive sustainable development, protect the environment, and foster meaningful cultural connections. Over the past ten years, I've honed my skills in digital storytelling, sharing narratives of travel experiences that prioritize environmental and social consciousness—without explicitly relying on the term 'sustainable.' My efforts extend to creating connections for social enterprises, linking markets from Guatemala to Goa.

I strive to explore the world in a distinctive manner—often embarking on solo journeys and venturing beyond typical tourist destinations to uncover the essence of a place. I bring these authentic, experiential, inspiring, and relatable stories from my travels to my readers. On both my blog and social platforms, I find my audience expressing their desire to follow in my footsteps wherever I venture.
